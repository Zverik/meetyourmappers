Meet Your Mapper

This page gives you a list of mappers around you with some basic mapping stats for each, so you can invite them to a mapping party or some other OSM event, or just say hello.

If you are interested in more detail about what other mappers are doing in your area, there are way better tools to do that, like OSMCha, Whodidit and Who's Around Me.

To get started, enter the OSM id for boundary relation representing your area of interest. It must be small-ish, because the data is fetched on-demand from Overpass.
